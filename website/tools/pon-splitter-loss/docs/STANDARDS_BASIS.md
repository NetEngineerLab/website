# Engineering basis

- GPON optical classes and PMD parameters follow ITU-T G.984.2.
- The default 1:8 × 1:8 cascade is evaluated with a GPON C+ profile.
- The 1490 nm attenuation value of 0.25 dB/km is an editable engineering typical value, not a normative universal maximum.
- ITU-T G.652 specifies fibre attenuation by wavelength regions and requires final design to account for cable, splice, connector and installation effects.
- Final projects must use actual equipment specifications, enterprise rules and field measurements.
